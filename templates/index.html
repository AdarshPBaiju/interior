About Us

WOODSTYLE FURNITURE & INTERIORS is an ISO 9001:2015 Certified interior designing firm in Thrissur, Chalakkudy. We do all kinds of furniture and interior works. We are proud in offering our customers exceptional service and value though. Woodstyle furniture and interiors is one of the pioneer in rendering turnkey interior solutions for residences and commercials,be it apartments, independent bungalows or villas, duplex or pent houses etc.., for the past 18 years in & around chalakkudy. We have in house teams of designers, decoraters and site supervisor who have wast experiences & exposures in handling varieties of residential projects. All our projects are executed by skilled terms of professional carpenters , painters,polishers,tile layers & other streams of workers etc.Apart from fresh interiors , we do excecute renovation works, alternation or modification works for already doneup spaces
